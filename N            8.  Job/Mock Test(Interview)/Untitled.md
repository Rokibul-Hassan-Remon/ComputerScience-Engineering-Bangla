# You are a fresher :
```cs
Use the Elevator Pitch Framework:

1. Start with a Hook - Begin with a unique statement describing your education or relevant project.

2. Skills & Interests - Combine your key skills with areas of interest.

3. Academic/Project Achievement - Share a notable academic or project accomplishment.

4. Career Aspiration - End with a brief statement on your career goals.

Example:

“I’m a driven Computer Science graduate with a passion for AI and machine learning. As my final-year project, I developed an AI-powered chatbot, achieving 95% accuracy in user interactions. Now I’m excited to apply my skills and knowledge to create strong impact in a dynamic organization.”

Give it a shot in your next intro & see how it makes you stand out.🙌🏻


```
